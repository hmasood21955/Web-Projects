Smartwatches

Smartwatches are wearable devices that offer a range of functionalities beyond timekeeping, such as fitness tracking, heart rate monitoring, and notifications for calls and messages. They sync with smartphones, providing easy access to apps and updates on the go. With sleek designs and customizable features, they cater to various styles and preferences. Many models also include GPS, music storage, and health-related features like ECG or blood oxygen monitoring.
